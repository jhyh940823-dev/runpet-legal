Article 1 (Purpose)

These terms govern Run Pet's location-based service for outdoor running GPS records.

Article 2 (Effect of terms)

These terms are provided through the app's public URL and in-app WebView. The last-updated date shown at the top of this document applies.

Article 3 (Service content)

During an outdoor run explicitly started by the user, Run Pet uses GPS to calculate route, distance, time, and pace. While an active run continues, location may be used within OS-permitted limits when the screen is locked or another app is open. Indoor running can be used without location.

Article 4 (Retention period)

Raw GPS route geometry is retained on the device for up to one year. V1 does not send routes to a Run Pet server or provide them to third parties.

Article 5 (Rights of the data subject)

Users can delete individual records or withdraw location consent in Settings. Withdrawal erases stored personal-location route geometry and prevents new outdoor GPS runs; game, pet, quest, and summary data remain.
