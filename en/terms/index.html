Run Pet combines run logging with pet gameplay. Users must be 14 years of age or older. It is not a medical, diagnostic, treatment, or disease-prevention service.

Account and sign-in

The launch target requires Apple or Google sign-in; guest play is not offered. First login may require acceptance of terms, privacy policy, and location-based service terms.

Data storage

Current release builds store primary data on-device and do not offer Run Pet server sync.

Distance, pace, and step counts are estimates from GPS and device sensors. Accuracy varies by environment and hardware; no outcome is guaranteed.
