Article 2 (Service provision)

GPS, pedometer, pace, distance, calories, route and voice guidance are convenience features. They are not medical, emergency, rescue, navigation, or fitness-safety services. Users must make safe outdoor-activity decisions, keep device/permission settings appropriate, and stop when a condition is unsafe.

Outdoor GPS running requires separate acceptance of the current location-based-service terms before OS location permission is requested.

Article 3 (Eligibility and accounts)

Run Pet is for users aged 14 and over. It does not offer guardian-consent verification and must not claim such verification.

V1 has no Run Pet server account.

Article 4 (User obligations)

Users must not misuse the service, interfere with its operation, reverse engineer where prohibited by law, or violate others' rights.

Article 5 (Content and data)

Current release builds store primary data on-device and do not offer Run Pet server sync.

Distance, pace, and step counts are estimates from GPS and device sensors. Location or sensor results can be inaccurate, delayed, unavailable, or lost.

Raw GPS route geometry is retained on the device for at most 365 days and can be redacted earlier by deleting a record or withdrawing location-terms consent. Local data can be lost through deletion, device loss, OS behavior, reinstall, or unavailable backup; V1 does not provide a Run Pet cloud backup.
