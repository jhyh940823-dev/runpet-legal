Run Pet Plus

Run Pet Plus is planned as monthly/yearly subscription. Product IDs and prices are pending store configuration.

Benefits (exactly four)

  1. Remove all ads
  2. Add photos to running records
  3. +2 daily food automatically
  4. +1 R currency on weekly quest claim

Food policy

Daily food is capped at 3; unused food does not carry over. Subscription convenience does not raise the daily cap.

Current build

  • StoreKit / Play Billing — not connected
  • Purchase CTA — coming soon (fail-closed)
  • AdMob — ad serving disabled in release (SDK may be in binary); ad-removal benefit is policy-planned

Subscription management

After IAP integration, cancel and restore guidance will point to iOS/Android subscription settings.
